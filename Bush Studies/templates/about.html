About Bush Studies

         Bush Studies was created as a senior project by Sivan Cooperman '19. He was inspired to create the website after performing a study with Math Department Chair Christine Miller, and hopes that it will encourage the school to continue to study and listen to the community. The platform is also available for, albeit not completely built for, teachers or students who wish to make their class work public.

         If you encounter any problems while using this website, please contact Sivan Cooperman at sivan.cooperman@bush.edu .
